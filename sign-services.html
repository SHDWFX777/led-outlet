Sign LED Retrofit, Repair & Disposal in Orlando

Old sign not lighting up? Flickering? Running up your electric bill? LED Outlet converts old fluorescent and neon sign lighting to LED. We replace the internals — LED modules, LED drivers (the power supply that converts high voltage to safe, low voltage), and wiring — so your existing sign looks brand new without building a new one.

We also repair signs that have gone dark. Bad driver, blown modules, wiring issues — we diagnose it and fix it on site with our own bucket truck. No need to hire a separate crane or lift company. And if your sign is beyond repair or you're ready to take it down, we handle full sign disposal too.

We're not a sign shop — we don't fabricate new signs. What we do is keep your existing signs lit, running on LED, and looking good. Call us and we'll give you a price over the phone.
